What others are asking:

  • What is CampusCash?

    • CampusCash is a prepaid debit card system for students to make payments and access funds

  • How do I load money onto CampusCash?

    • Funds are loaded monthly as part of student tuition, alternatively students can Top up their
    account by directly depositing money at the bank with their student number as the reference

  • Where can I use CampusCash?

    • CampusCash can be used at participating merchants STRICTLY on campus

  • Is CampusCash safe?

    • Yes, it's protected by PIN and secure systems

  • Can I withdraw cash with CampusCash?

    • No, the purpose of CampusCash is to eliminate "Cash" on campus

  • What if I lose my student card?

    • Report it immediately to get a replacement
