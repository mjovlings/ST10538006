About CampusCash

CampusCash offers a seamless, cashless payment solution for Rosebank College students to purchase lunch, enhancing their campus experience.
By integrating with the existing student card system, we provide a convenient, secure, and efficient payment method. Our solution is designed
to increase student satisfaction, reduce operational costs, and generate revenue for the college.

Mission

To provide a convenient, secure, and efficient payment solution that meets the evolving needs of students, while driving growth and innovation
in the edtech and fintech space.

Vision

To revolutionize the way students pay for meals and create a seamless, cashless campus
experience that enhances student life.
